Manual of Me

CONDITIONS I LIKE TO WORK IN?

  • Organized and structured yet calm environment
  • I like to listen to music whilst I work but I do find hard to concentrate if there’s a lot of noise and distractions
  • Strong learning culture, where asking questions is welcomed

TIMES/HOURS I LIKE TO WORK?

  • I find myself more productive in the mornings and late evenings

THE BEST WAY TO COMMUNICATE WITH ME?

  • Through messages (for example, slack/ telegram or other form of messaging systems) I rarely pick up my phone but I look at text messages I also check my e-mail quite often during the day
  • Verbally, I like people to communicate in a calm, adequate manner in a two way system, where there’s active listening and then discussion of ideas.
